Who is Jensen Huang?

Jensen Huang is one of the co-founders for Nvidia, founding it in 1993. His company invented the first GPU (Graphics Processing Unit) that was commercially available for consumers to buy. Jensen Huang is a very famous historical figure in computer science because of his GPU line-up. These pieces of technology bring us closer to artificial intelligence, deep A.I learning and autonomous cars. Nvidia shovels the path towards artificial intelligence by being the global leader in graphics processing technology.
